Philippine 2022 Election

Vote Wisely!


Candidates can be evaluated through their opinions on societal issues, their government experience and their leadership skills that they would bring to office, that is why it is must to provide the voters a credible information about the people who are running to become the nation's leaders.

knowyourcandidate.com intends to assist voters in determining who is deserving of their vote by providing them a website that would show the candidates' stands on social issues, their academic history and credentials. The website will feature the presidential and vice presidential candidates, as well as those who are running for governors and mayors in their respective LGUs.
